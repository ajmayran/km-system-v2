BAMBOO

A fast-growing plant with versatile uses, including construction, furniture-making, and handicrafts. It is also an eco-friendly material with exceptional strength and sustainability benefits. Bamboo can grow up to 91 cm (3 ft) in a single day and reaches maturity in just 3-5 years, making it one of the most renewable building materials available.
