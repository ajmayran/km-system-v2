Project Rationale

This project addresses the pressing need for inclusive agricultural growth and sustainable livelihood opportunities in the region through a participatory, community-based, and innovation-driven approach that strengthens productivity and ecological balance.

Project Objectives

Our comprehensive objectives align with national priorities for inclusive and sustainable agriculture.

Increase Agricultural Productivity

  • Deploy precision technologies and sustainable practices in farming systems
  • Promote the use of smart agriculture tools
  • Support crop diversification and high-value crops

Productivity Targets

  • 30% increase in crop yields within 3 years
  • 50% reduction in post-harvest losses
  • 100 demonstration farms established

Strengthen Livelihood Opportunities

  • Develop community-based agri-enterprises
  • Train farmers in agribusiness and entrepreneurship
  • Enhance access to financial and market linkages

Livelihood Targets

  • 50 farmer cooperatives organized
  • 5,000 farmers trained in business skills
  • 20 new market linkages established

Empower Local Communities

  • Facilitate participatory planning and decision-making
  • Promote gender and youth inclusion in agriculture
  • Build capacity through extension and training programs

Empowerment Targets

  • 100 community action plans developed
  • 40% women participation in all programs
  • 30 youth agri-entrepreneurs supported

Promote Environmental Sustainability

  • Advocate climate-smart agriculture practices
  • Rehabilitate degraded lands and promote agroforestry
  • Support biodiversity conservation efforts

Sustainability Targets

  • 10,000 hectares under sustainable practices
  • 500 hectares of degraded land restored
  • 25% reduction in chemical inputs
